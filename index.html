A City Built for Walking

Welcome to Dublin City Walks

Dublin is a vibrant city that offers the best of both worlds when it comes to hitting the tarmac. Known as a thriving metropolis and also a haven for nature-lovers, there's plenty of range to roam and a great opportunity for an adventurous walker.

There are a wealth of fun and dynamic walks to be experienced in Dublin. Walks dedicated to the city centre will take you to many a famous landmark. These include the General Post Office and the 1916 Rebellion, Dublin Castle, Trinity College, Old Parliament, Hoggen Green, Christ Church Cathedral, Old City Walls, Dublin's Medieval and Viking heritage and much more.

The City Highlights Walking tour is your essential guide to Dublin city. Visit some of the Iconic landmarks, meet the friendly locals, partake in Gaelic sports and language demonstrations and more. - A fun and dynamic walk, an experience like no other.
